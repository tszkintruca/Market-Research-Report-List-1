<p><p>Basket centrifuges are essential in industrial separation processes, categorized primarily into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical basket centrifuges utilize a cylindrical basket positioned vertically, optimizing sedimentation and offering high efficiency in separating solids from liquids. In contrast, horizontal basket centrifuges feature a horizontally oriented basket, which facilitates better sediment handling and allows for continuous operation, making them suitable for large-scale applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
