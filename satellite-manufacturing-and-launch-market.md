<p><p>Satellite manufacturing and launch markets can be categorized into four segments: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Geostationary Orbit (GEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Beyond GEO. LEO is favored for broadband and Earth observation, offering low latency. GEO satellites provide stable communication links and extensive coverage, ideal for telecommunications and broadcasting. MEO is typically used for navigation systems like GPS. Beyond GEO encompasses missions aimed at exploring space beyond Earth's orbit, including deep space exploration and science missions, requiring specialized technology and higher investment.</p></p>
