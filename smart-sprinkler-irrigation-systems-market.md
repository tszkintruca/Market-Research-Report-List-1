<p><strong>The Smart Sprinkler Irrigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weather-based Control</li><li>Sensor-based Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart sprinkler irrigation systems come in two primary market types: weather-based control and sensor-based control. Weather-based control systems adjust watering schedules based on real-time weather data, optimizing water usage by avoiding unnecessary irrigation during rainfall or high humidity. In contrast, sensor-based control systems utilize soil moisture sensors to measure the moisture level in the ground, ensuring that water is applied only when necessary. Both types enhance efficiency, conserve water resources, and promote healthier plant growth.</p></p>
