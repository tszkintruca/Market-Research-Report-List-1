<p><p>The textile detergent market consists of three primary types: powder, liquid, and solid formulations. Liquid detergents are convenient for pre-treatment and dissolving easily, making them ideal for cold washes. Each type caters to specific consumer preferences and washing needs, contributing to a diverse market landscape.</p></p>
