Synthetic hair brushes, made from materials like nylon or polyester, are preferred for their durability, vegan-friendly nature, and ease of cleaning, making them ideal for cream and liquid products. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and application techniques in the beauty industry.</p></p>
