<p><p>The adhesive wound dressing market comprises various types designed for specific healing needs. Hydrophilic colloid dressings promote a moist environment, accelerating healing for moderate exudate wounds. Alginic acid dressings, derived from seaweed, absorb significant exudate and form a gel to facilitate healing. Transparent film dressings provide a protective barrier while allowing visibility of the wound, ideal for low-exudate situations. Other types include foam dressings and hydrogel dressings, each serving unique functions in wound management based on exudate levels and wound types.</p></p>
