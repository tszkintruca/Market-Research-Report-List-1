<p><p>The Food Allergy Therapeutics Market comprises three primary types: Epinephrine injection, antihistamines, and other therapies. Epinephrine injections are critical for rapid treatment of severe allergic reactions, providing immediate relief by constricting blood vessels and opening airways. Antihistamines help alleviate mild symptoms by blocking histamine release, reducing itchiness and swelling. Other market options may include immunotherapy or emerging treatments aimed at desensitization, which focus on long-term management of allergies by gradually increasing tolerance to specific allergens.</p></p>
