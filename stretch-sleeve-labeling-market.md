<p><p>Stretch sleeve labeling is a packaging technique that involves using highly elastic sleeves made from materials like polyethylene or PVC, which are heat-shrinkable and conform to the shape of the container. Stretch sleeve labels are lightweight, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly, making them popular in various industries, particularly food and beverages, personal care, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The Stretch Sleeve Labeling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer demand for attractive and informative packaging solutions.
