<p><strong>The Passive UHF Inlay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UHF Dry Inlay</li><li>UHF Wet Inlay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passive UHF inlay market is divided into two main types: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays consist of a hardware component embedded within a flexible adhesive that allows for easy application on various surfaces, while UHF wet inlays are manufactured using a more complex process, involving the application of the inlay to a substrate, often resulting in a stronger bond. Both types are widely used in logistics, inventory management, and retail applications for efficient tracking and identification.</p></p>
