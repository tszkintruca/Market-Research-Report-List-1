<p><ul><li>Distributed Feedback Laser</li><li>Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser</li><li>Fabry-perot Laser</li><li>Broad Area Laser Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are semiconductor lasers characterized by their light emission from the edge of the laser chip. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB)** provides wavelength stability through a periodic structure. </p><p>2. **Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser (DBR)** uses mirrors to enhance performance. </p><p>3. **Fabry-Perot Laser** offers simple design and cost-effectiveness, with multiple resonant modes. </p><p>4. **Broad Area Laser Diodes** deliver high power over a larger area but may have lower beam quality. These types cater to diverse applications, including telecommunications and industrial uses.</p></p>
