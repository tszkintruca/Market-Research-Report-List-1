<p><p>Exhaust abatement systems are classified into various types based on their operating mechanisms. Combustion type systems use high temperatures to incinerate pollutants, effectively reducing harmful emissions. Wet methods involve scrubbing exhaust gases with liquids, trapping contaminants for disposal. Dry methods utilize sorbents and filters to capture pollutants without requiring water, making them ideal in water-scarce regions. Other systems may include innovative technologies like plasma treatments or biofiltration, enhancing overall efficiency in managing exhaust emissions across various industries.</p></p>
