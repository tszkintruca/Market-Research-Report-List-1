<p><ul><li>Full Embedding Stimulator</li><li>Semi-embedding Stimulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal cord stimulator market is categorized into full embedding and semi-embedding stimulators. Full embedding stimulators are implanted entirely within the body, offering greater discretion and reducing the risk of device visibility or external damage. In contrast, semi-embedding stimulators partially reside under the skin, balancing ease of access for adjustments and maintenance while still providing therapeutic benefits. Both types focus on alleviating chronic pain by modulating neural activity but cater to different patient preferences and clinical needs.</p></p>
