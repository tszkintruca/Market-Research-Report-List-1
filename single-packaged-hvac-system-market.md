<p><p>The Single Packaged HVAC System market includes two main types: air-cooled systems and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled systems use ambient air for heat exchange, offering easy installation and lower maintenance, making them ideal for residential and light commercial applications. Conversely, water-cooled systems rely on water as a cooling medium, providing higher efficiency and better performance in larger installations or commercial buildings. Each type serves distinct needs, with air-cooled systems being more prevalent in smaller settings and water-cooled systems favored in industrial environments.</p></p>
