<p><p>The Counter Drone System market consists of two main types: Detection Systems and Neutralizing Systems. Detection Systems focus on identifying unauthorized drones using technologies like radar, radio frequency (RF) detection, and optical sensors, enabling real-time tracking. Neutralizing Systems, on the other hand, aim to disable or capture drones through various methods, including jamming signals, deploying nets, or using directed energy weapons. Together, these systems are essential for managing drone threats in security-sensitive areas such as military, government, and public spaces.</p></p>
