<p><p>The Automatic Transfer Switch Controller (ATSC) market is segmented into panel-mounted and DIN rail mounted types. Panel-mounted ATSCs are designed for installation directly onto control panels, offering ease of use and visibility for operators. They are ideal for fixed installations in larger systems. In contrast, DIN rail mounted controllers are compact and designed to fit onto standard DIN rails, making them suitable for smaller enclosures and more versatile setups. Both types enhance power management and enable seamless transitions during power outages.</p></p>
