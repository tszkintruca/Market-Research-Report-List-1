<p><strong>The PPS Composite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-linked Type PPS Composite</li><li>Linear Type PPS Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS composite materials are categorized into two main types: cross-linked and linear. Cross-linked PPS composites incorporate a network of interconnected polymer chains, enhancing thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, linear PPS composites consist of parallel polymer chains, offering better flexibility and processability, ideal for applications requiring easy shaping and molding. Both types leverage the inherent properties of PPS to meet diverse industrial needs, ranging from aerospace to electronics.</p></p>
