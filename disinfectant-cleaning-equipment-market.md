<p><p>The disinfectant cleaning equipment market is categorized into chemical and physical methods. Chemical methods involve the use of various disinfectant agents, such as alcohols, chlorine compounds, and quaternary ammonium compounds, to eliminate pathogens on surfaces. These are widely used in healthcare, hospitality, and food industries. Physical methods include techniques like steam cleaning, ultraviolet (UV) light exposure, and thermal disinfection, which use heat or radiation to destroy microbes without chemicals. Both methods aim to maintain hygiene and prevent infection.</p></p>
