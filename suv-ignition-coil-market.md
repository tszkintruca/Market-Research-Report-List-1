<p><strong>The SUV Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV ignition coil market is primarily categorized into two types: single-spark and multi-spark coils. Single-spark coils generate one spark per ignition cycle, providing adequate performance for standard engines. In contrast, multi-spark coils deliver multiple sparks during each cycle, improving combustion efficiency and engine responsiveness, especially in high-performance vehicles. These distinctions cater to different consumer preferences, with single-spark coils generally found in conventional models while multi-spark options are favored in more advanced and sporty SUVs, enhancing overall driving dynamics.</p></p>
