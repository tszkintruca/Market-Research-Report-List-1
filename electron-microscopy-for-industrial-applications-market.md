<p><p>Electron microscopy is a powerful imaging technique used in various industrial applications to analyze materials at the microscopic level. The market for electron microscopy primarily includes Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM), Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), and other specialized techniques. TEM provides high-resolution images by transmitting electrons through samples, ideal for internal structure analysis. SEM offers detailed surface topography and composition by scanning samples with focused electron beams. Other methods encompass techniques like Electron Backscatter Diffraction (EBSD), expanding analytical capabilities across industries such as materials science, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
