<p><p>The RT-PCR test kits market is primarily categorized into one-step and two-step types. One-step kits streamline the process by combining reverse transcription and PCR amplification into a single reaction, enhancing efficiency and reducing contamination risk. In contrast, two-step kits involve separate reverse transcription followed by PCR amplification, allowing for more flexibility in reaction conditions but often requiring more time and resources. Both types serve essential roles in diagnostics, particularly for infectious diseases and genetic testing.</p></p>
