<p><p>The voltage supervisors market is segmented into two primary types: surface mounting and through-hole. Surface mount voltage supervisors are designed for compact, high-density applications, allowing for seamless integration into printed circuit boards (PCBs) with automated assembly processes. In contrast, through-hole voltage supervisors are preferred for rugged applications, offering strong mechanical support and easier manual assembly. Each type caters to different needs, with surface mount favored for modern devices and through-hole for traditional or demanding environments.</p></p>
