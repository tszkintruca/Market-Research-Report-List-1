<p><p>The Terahertz sensors market comprises primarily two types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing utilizes light waves in the terahertz range to detect materials and substances, offering high sensitivity and non-destructive testing capabilities. In contrast, electronic sensing employs electronic devices to measure terahertz signals, enabling compact, cost-effective solutions for applications such as imaging and spectroscopy. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, security, and biomedical fields, driving innovation and advancements in detection technologies.</p></p>
