<p><ul><li>EPS Foaming Mould</li><li>EPP Foaming Mould</li><li>PU Foaming Mould</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foaming mould market is categorized primarily into EPS (Expanded Polystyrene), EPP (Expanded Polypropylene), and PU (Polyurethane) foaming moulds. EPS foaming moulds are lightweight and offer excellent thermal insulation, making them ideal for packaging and construction. EPP foaming moulds provide superior impact resistance and are often used in automotive and packaging applications. PU foaming moulds are versatile, known for their durability and flexibility, commonly utilized in furniture and insulation products. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
