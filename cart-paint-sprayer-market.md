<p><strong>The Cart Paint Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HVLP</li><li>Airless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cart paint sprayer market is primarily divided into two types: HVLP (High Volume Low Pressure) and airless sprayers. HVLP sprayers use high air volume at low pressure, delivering a fine finish with less overspray, making them ideal for detailed work. In contrast, airless sprayers operate at high pressure, allowing for faster coverage of large surfaces, suitable for exterior projects and thicker paints. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to both professional contractors and DIY enthusiasts.</p></p>
