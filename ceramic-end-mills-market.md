<p><ul><li>Iron-based</li><li>Nickel-based</li><li>Cobalt-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic end mills market is categorized into three primary types based on the metal matrix used: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based. Iron-based ceramic end mills offer high wear resistance and are suitable for cutting ferrous materials. Nickel-based variants excel in high-temperature applications due to their thermal stability, making them ideal for exotic alloys. Cobalt-based ceramic end mills are known for their hardness and toughness, making them effective in machining tough materials. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, enhancing productivity and performance.</p></p>
