<p><p>The body armor vest market comprises two main types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor is made from flexible materials like Kevlar, offering protection against handgun rounds and fragmentation while being lightweight and comfortable for daily wear. In contrast, hard armor consists of rigid plates made from materials like ceramic or polyethylene, providing superior defense against higher-caliber firearms and armor-piercing rounds. Both types cater to different needs, balancing protection levels with mobility and usability in various environments.</p></p>
