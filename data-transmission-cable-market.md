<p><p>The Data Transmission Cable Market is primarily categorized into two types based on shielding: Copper Braid Shield and Foil Shield. Copper Braid Shield cables utilize a woven copper mesh to protect against electromagnetic interference, offering robust durability and flexibility, ideal for environments with high noise levels. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs across various industries.</p></p>
