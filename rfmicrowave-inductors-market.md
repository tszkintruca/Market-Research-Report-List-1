<p><p>The RFMicrowave Inductors market features three primary types: Wire Wound, Film, and Multilayer inductors. Wire Wound inductors utilize coiled wire to achieve high inductance values and handle significant power, making them suitable for various applications. Film inductors employ thin layers of conductive materials, offering precision and compact designs, ideal for high-frequency applications. Multilayer inductors stack multiple layers of materials to enhance performance in space-constrained environments, providing high inductance in a small footprint. Each type is tailored for specific technical requirements in RF applications.</p></p>
