<p><p>The compartment interior coating market is primarily segmented into basecoat and topcoat categories. Basecoat serves as the foundational layer, providing adhesion, protection, and a smooth surface for subsequent coatings. It enhances durability and aesthetics. The topcoat, applied over the basecoat, offers additional benefits such as glossiness, color, and resistance to environmental factors. Together, these coatings contribute to the overall appearance, protection, and longevity of interior compartments in vehicles, enhancing both functionality and design appeal.</p></p>
