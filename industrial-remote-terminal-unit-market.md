<p><p>The industrial remote terminal unit (RTU) market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired industrial RTUs. Wireless industrial RTUs utilize radio frequency, cellular, or satellite communications, enabling real-time data transmission without the constraints of physical cabling, which enhances flexibility and reduces installation costs. In contrast, wired industrial RTUs rely on traditional communication protocols over physical cables, offering stable and secure connections, often favored in environments where reliability and minimal interference are critical for operational efficiency.</p></p>
