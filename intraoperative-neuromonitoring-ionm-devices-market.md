<p><p>The intraoperative neuromonitoring (IONM) devices market encompasses various types including EEG, EMG, and EP monitoring. EEG monitoring tracks electrical brain activity to identify potential neurological issues during surgery. EMG monitoring assesses muscle electrical activity, helping to detect nerve function and prevent injury. EP monitoring evaluates the electrical signals in the sensory pathways, ensuring the integrity of neural structures. Together, these modalities enhance patient safety and facilitate surgical interventions by providing real-time feedback on the nervous system's status.</p></p>
