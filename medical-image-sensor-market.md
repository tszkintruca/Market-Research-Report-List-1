<p><strong>The Medical Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical image sensor market primarily includes two types: Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for applications requiring precise imaging, such as in endoscopy. Conversely, CMOS sensors offer advantages like lower power consumption, faster readout speeds, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for portable imaging devices. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
