<p><p>The passive microwave devices market consists of various frequency bands, each serving specific applications. The L-band (1-2 GHz) is used in communication and GPS. C-band (4-8 GHz) is popular for satellite and radar systems. S-band (2-4 GHz) finds use in weather radars and mobile communication. X-band (8-12 GHz) is employed in aerospace and defense. Ku-band (12-18 GHz) is significant for satellite communications. Ka-band (26.5-40 GHz) offers high data rates for advanced communication systems. Other bands include V-band and Q-band, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
