<p><strong>The Parasitic Infection Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microscopy</li><li>Serologic Tests</li><li>Molecular Based Assays</li><li>Proteomics Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parasitic infection testing market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Microscopy** involves direct visualization of parasites in samples, such as blood or stool, using optical microscopes.</p><p>2. **Serologic Tests** detect antibodies or antigens related to specific parasites in the blood, offering insights into immune responses.</p><p>3. **Molecular-Based Assays** utilize techniques like PCR to identify parasite DNA, providing high sensitivity and specificity.</p><p>4. **Proteomics Testing** analyzes the proteins produced by parasites for better diagnostics and treatment monitoring, enhancing understanding of host-parasite interactions.</p></p>
