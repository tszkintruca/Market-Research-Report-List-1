<p><p>Freeze granulation is a technique used to create granules by combining freeze-drying and granulation processes. This method involves suspending solid particles in a cryogenic medium, where they are frozen before undergoing sublimation, resulting in the formation of porous granules. Freeze granulation is particularly advantageous for pharmaceuticals and biotechnology, as it enhances the stability and bioavailability of products.</p><p>The Freeze Granulation Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-quality pharmaceutical formulations and advancements in drug delivery systems.
