<p><p>The Smart Wireless Smoke Detector market primarily consists of two types: Ionic Smoke Alarms and Photoelectric Smoke Alarms. Ionic Smoke Alarms use radioactive materials to detect smoke particles, offering fast detection of flaming fires but may be less effective with smoldering fires. In contrast, Photoelectric Smoke Alarms utilize a light sensor and a light source to identify smoke, making them more responsive to smoldering fires. Both types enhance safety by integrating smart technology for real-time alerts and monitoring.</p></p>
