<p><p>Smart HVAC controls can be categorized into short-range and long-distance markets based on their operational scope and connectivity. Short-range smart HVAC controls typically operate within a localized environment, utilizing technologies like Bluetooth and Zigbee for communication between devices such as thermostats and sensors, ensuring efficient energy consumption in smaller spaces. In contrast, long-distance market solutions rely on Wi-Fi and cellular networks to enable remote access and monitoring, allowing users to control HVAC systems from anywhere, enhancing comfort and energy management on a larger scale.</p></p>
