<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi-permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional hair dye market offers three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide a short-term color solution that typically washes out after a few shampoos. Semi-permanent dyes last longer, usually fading over several weeks without requiring a strong developer, making them ideal for a natural look or enhancing existing color. Permanent hair dyes, on the other hand, provide long-lasting color through a chemical process that penetrates the hair shaft, offering vibrant and enduring results.</p></p>
