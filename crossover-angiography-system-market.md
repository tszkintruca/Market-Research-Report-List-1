<p><strong>The Crossover Angiography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Angiography System</li><li>CT Angiography System</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Angiography System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crossover Angiography System market is segmented into three main types: X-ray Angiography Systems, CT Angiography Systems, and Magnetic Resonance Angiography Systems. X-ray Angiography uses fluoroscopic imaging to visualize blood vessels in real-time, making it ideal for interventional procedures. CT Angiography employs computed tomography to provide detailed cross-sectional images of blood vessels, offering enhanced anatomical visualization. Magnetic Resonance Angiography utilizes magnetic resonance imaging to non-invasively assess vascular structures, providing high-contrast images without ionizing radiation, appealing for patients requiring detailed vascular diagnostics.</p></p>
