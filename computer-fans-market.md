<p><ul><li>Chassis Fan</li><li>CPU Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer fans market can be divided primarily into chassis fans and CPU fans. Chassis fans are designed to enhance airflow within the computer case, ensuring optimal cooling for all components. They help maintain stable temperatures, prevent overheating, and enhance system longevity. On the other hand, CPU fans are specifically tailored to cool the processor, dissipating heat generated during operation. Both types are essential for maintaining overall system performance, reliability, and energy efficiency in modern computing environments.</p></p>
