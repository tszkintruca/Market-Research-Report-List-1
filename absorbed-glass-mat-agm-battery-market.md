<p><p>Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries are a type of sealed lead-acid battery characterized by their use of a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, which enhances performance and safety. AGM batteries are known for their low self-discharge rates, robustness, and ability to withstand deep cycles, making them suitable for a variety of applications, including in renewable energy systems, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), and automotive uses.</p><p>The AGM battery market is experiencing significant growth, projected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. Additionally, the trend towards sustainable energy solutions and advancements in battery technologies are encouraging the adoption of AGM batteries. Key market trends include the emergence of improved battery designs, enhanced recycling processes, and a growing emphasis on environmentally friendly practices.
