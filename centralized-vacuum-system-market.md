<p><p>The centralized vacuum system market encompasses two main types: pipeline and built-in systems. </p><p>Pipeline systems utilize extensive tubing installed throughout a building to transport dirt and debris to a central collection unit, offering convenience and efficiency. In contrast, built-in systems integrate vacuuming capabilities directly into the structure, often including retractable hoses and in-wall outlets, enhancing aesthetics and usability. Both types provide effective cleaning solutions, but cater to different needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
