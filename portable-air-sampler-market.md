<p><strong>The Portable Air Sampler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable air sampler market comprises two main types: active and passive. Active air samplers utilize battery-operated fans to draw air through a filter, capturing airborne particles, making them ideal for precise measurements in various environments. In contrast, passive air samplers rely on natural air movement to collect samples over time, offering a cost-effective and maintenance-friendly solution. Both types serve essential roles in air quality monitoring, environmental studies, and occupational health, catering to diverse user needs and applications.</p></p>
