<p><p>The semiconductor IP license market comprises three main types: soft cores, hard cores, and firm cores. Soft cores are flexible, synthesizable designs that can be customized for various applications. Hard cores are physical implementations that are optimized for performance but are less flexible. Firm cores offer a middle ground, featuring a predefined layout that retains some customization options while ensuring efficient integration. Each type serves different design needs, balancing between flexibility, performance, and integration ease in semiconductor development.</p></p>
