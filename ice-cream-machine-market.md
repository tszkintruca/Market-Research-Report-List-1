<p><p>The ice cream machine market is primarily divided into two types: soft ice cream machines and hard ice cream machines. Soft ice cream machines produce creamy, air-infused ice cream that is served directly from the machine, making it ideal for soft serve establishments. In contrast, hard ice cream machines create denser ice cream that requires freezing after mixing, suitable for scooping in traditional ice cream parlors. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and operational needs within the frozen dessert industry.</p></p>
