<p><p>Scanning Probe Microscopy (SPM) encompasses advanced imaging techniques utilizing a physical probe to scan the surface of materials at the nanoscale. Key market types include Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM), which measure surface forces and topography; Scanning Tunneling Microscopes (STM), which leverage electron tunneling between the probe and surface for high-resolution imaging; and Near-field Scanning Optical Microscopes (NSOM), which allow optical imaging beyond diffraction limits by using a nanoscale probe. These technologies cater to diverse applications in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
