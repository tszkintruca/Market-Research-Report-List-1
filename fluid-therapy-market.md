<p><p>Fluid therapy encompasses various market types, primarily classified into colloids, crystalloids, and blood products. Colloid solutions contain larger molecules that help maintain oncotic pressure, making them useful for treating hypovolemia. Crystalloids consist of small molecules and are used to replenish fluids and electrolytes. Blood products, including whole blood, plasma, and red cell concentrates, are crucial for severe trauma or surgical procedures. Each type plays a vital role in medical settings, addressing different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
