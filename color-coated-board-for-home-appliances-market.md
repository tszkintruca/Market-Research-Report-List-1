<p><p>Color Coated Boards for home appliances primarily include two types: PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal). PCM features a layer of paint applied directly to metal substrates, offering good aesthetic appeal and corrosion resistance. VCM involves a vinyl film laminated onto the metal surface, providing enhanced durability, flexibility, and a wide range of colors and textures. These boards are widely used in products like refrigerators, washing machines, and other household items, combining functionality with modern design appeal.</p></p>
