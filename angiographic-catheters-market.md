<p><p>The angiographic catheters market comprises several types, each designed for specific vascular interventions. Scoring balloon catheters possess longitudinal score lines to enhance arterial expansion, and conventional balloon catheters are standard for non-complex dilations. Each type addresses different clinical needs, contributing to improved patient outcomes in cardiovascular treatments and expanding their application across various medical specialties.</p></p>
