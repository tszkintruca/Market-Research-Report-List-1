<p><ul><li>CVD Silicon Carbide Coated Graphite</li><li>CVD Pyrolytic Graphite Coated Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CVD coated graphite market comprises two main types: CVD silicon carbide coated graphite and CVD pyrolytic graphite coated graphite. CVD silicon carbide coated graphite exhibits enhanced wear resistance and thermal stability, making it ideal for high-temperature applications and harsh environments. In contrast, CVD pyrolytic graphite coated graphite offers superior electrical conductivity and low permeability, often utilized in semiconductor and aerospace industries. Both types leverage chemical vapor deposition techniques to enhance the properties of graphite for specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
