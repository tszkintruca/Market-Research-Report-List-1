<p><p>The CT imaging market encompasses various modalities, primarily X-ray CT, PET, and SPECT. X-ray CT uses X-ray technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in diagnostics. PET imaging detects radioactive tracers to assess metabolic functions, useful for oncology and neurology. SPECT, similar to PET, employs gamma rays to visualize physiological processes, often in cardiac and brain studies. These technologies are essential in diagnosing diseases, planning treatments, and monitoring patient progress across various medical fields.</p></p>
