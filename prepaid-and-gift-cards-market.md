<p><strong>The Prepaid and Gift Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepaid and gift card market can be categorized into two primary types: open loop and closed loop. Open loop cards are issued by major payment networks like Visa or Mastercard, allowing users to spend at various merchants that accept those networks. Closed loop cards, on the other hand, are restricted to a specific retailer or group of retailers, enabling users to spend only at designated locations. Both types cater to different consumer needs and spending patterns.</p></p>
