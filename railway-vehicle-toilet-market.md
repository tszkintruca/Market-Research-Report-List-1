<p><p>The railway vehicle toilet market primarily offers two types of systems: vacuum toilet systems and pressured water flushing toilet systems. Vacuum toilet systems use low water volume and create suction to flush waste, making them efficient and suitable for high-speed trains. In contrast, pressured water flushing toilet systems rely on a pressurized water supply to flush waste, providing a more traditional approach. Both systems aim to enhance cleanliness and hygiene in railway vehicles while addressing space constraints and resource management.</p></p>
